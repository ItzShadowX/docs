# Donut Crates Operations

## Usage and Operations

1. Create a small internal test crate first.
2. Issue test keys to staff accounts.
3. Open crates in a staging environment and verify rewards resolve correctly.
4. Confirm rare rewards, duplicate handling, and edge-case outcomes.
5. Move the final crate definitions to production once the reward pool is validated.

## Troubleshooting

### Crates open but rewards fail

Check for:

- Invalid reward syntax in configuration
- Missing item definitions or dependent plugins
- Permissions blocking the final action

### Keys are not recognized

Verify key identifiers, matching crate names, and any permission or metadata requirements used by the plugin.

### Reward balance feels off

Audit reward weights and remove extreme-value items until you have real usage data from production players.
