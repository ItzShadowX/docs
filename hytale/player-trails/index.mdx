# Player Trails

Player Trails adds cosmetic trail effects for players in Hytale environments. It is designed for creators who want lightweight visual progression, premium cosmetics, or event-driven rewards without disrupting core gameplay systems.

## What This Plugin Covers

- Cosmetic trail effects for player presentation
- Flexible access control through permissions or progression
- Controlled rollout for seasonal, premium, or achievement-based rewards
- Straightforward admin maintenance and effect tuning
