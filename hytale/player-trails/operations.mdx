# Player Trails Operations

## Usage and Operations

1. Assign a small test set of trails to internal accounts.
2. Review visibility in crowded environments.
3. Validate equip, unequip, and switching behavior.
4. Confirm restricted trails remain inaccessible to standard users.
5. Monitor runtime behavior during the first live event or release window.

## Troubleshooting

### Effects do not appear

Check plugin startup logs, trail enablement settings, and player access requirements.

### Performance degrades in busy areas

Reduce active effect density, simplify enabled cosmetics, or narrow access while testing optimized values.

### Players can access the wrong cosmetics

Audit permission mappings, unlock conditions, and any inherited role assignments from your server setup.
